About

Trojan Whore is a one-man extreme music project blending elements of cybergrind, pornogrind, and goregrind into a bass-driven assault with no synths and no genre rules. All riffs and melodies are performed on bass guitar, pushed through pedals and effects to create a mechanical, hostile sound that borders on noise. Influenced by black metal’s cold atmosphere and sludge metal’s oppressive weight, Trojan Whore moves between blast-heavy chaos, filthy slow crawls, and warped low-end melodies. The result is abrasive, confrontational, and intentionally uncomfortable.
